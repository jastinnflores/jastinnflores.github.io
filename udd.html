You do not need to be logged into the app to request deletion. You can initiate the deletion process using either of the following methods:

  • By Email: Send an explicit request to delete your account and data to our official support email: avishinnovationsceo@gmail.com.

Timeframe: Upon receiving your request via email, your account and associated data will be permanently reviewed and deleted within 72 hours.

What Data is Deleted

When you request account deletion, all personal data associated with your account in [Your App Name] will be permanently removed from our databases. This includes your profile information, authentication credentials, and any user-generated data linked to your account.

Conditions and Exceptions for Data Retention

Please be aware that we may retain certain information even after an account deletion request if it is strictly necessary to comply with legal, regulatory, or security obligations, including:

  • Completion of pending financial transactions.
  • Contractual obligations or fraud prevention/security purposes.
  • Compliance with applicable local and international privacy laws.

Your Additional Rights (Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising)

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising on mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your device:

Android Device Settings:

  1. Open device Settings
  2. Select Google
  3. Select Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (or "Delete advertising ID" depending on your Android version).
